# 🎮 Project Trinity

> Every Crime Has Three Stories.

Project Trinity is a narrative-driven crime simulation game where players experience the same criminal case from three unique perspectives:

- 👤 Victim
- 🕶️ Perpetrator
- 🕵️ Investigator

Unlike traditional detective games, every major character is playable. Every decision changes the outcome of the case, making every playthrough unique.

---

# 📖 Concept

A crime occurs.

The player chooses one role to experience the case.

Each role has different objectives, information, abilities, and possible endings.

The complete truth is revealed only after playing all three perspectives.

---

# 🎭 Playable Roles

## 👤 Victim

The victim is not always helpless.

Possible Victim Classes

- Civilian
- Police Officer
- Detective
- Mafia Member
- Business Owner
- Journalist
- Doctor
- Scientist
- Hacker
- Politician

### Objectives

- Survive
- Escape
- Protect evidence
- Help investigators
- Stop the perpetrator if possible

Every victim has different abilities depending on their profession.

---

## 🕶️ Perpetrator

The player becomes the criminal.

Possible Classes

- Contract Killer
- Mafia Boss
- Assassin
- Hacker
- Smuggler
- Spy
- Corrupt Officer
- Corporate Criminal

### Objectives

- Complete the mission
- Escape
- Hide evidence
- Mislead investigators
- Eliminate witnesses

The player decides

- When to attack
- How to attack
- Whether to fight
- Whether to flee
- Whether to surrender

---

## 🕵️ Investigator

The player investigates the crime.

Possible Investigator Classes

- Detective
- Crime Branch
- Intelligence Officer
- Cyber Crime Officer
- Forensic Expert

### Objectives

- Collect evidence
- Examine crime scenes
- Interview witnesses
- Analyze CCTV
- Build timelines
- Identify suspects
- Arrest the perpetrator

---

# ⚔ Dynamic Gameplay

Every encounter has multiple outcomes.

## Victim vs Perpetrator

Possible Results

- Victim escapes
- Victim defeats perpetrator
- Perpetrator succeeds
- Both survive
- Both are injured

## Investigator vs Perpetrator

Possible Results

- Arrest
- Escape
- Surrender
- Shootout
- Hand-to-hand combat

No outcome is predetermined.

---

# 🤖 AI & Multiplayer

## Single Player

Player chooses one role.

Other roles are controlled by AI.

Example

Player → Investigator

Victim → AI

Perpetrator → AI

---

## Two Player Mode

Player 1 → Investigator

Player 2 → Perpetrator

Victim → AI

OR

Player 1 → Victim

Player 2 → Perpetrator

Investigator → AI

---

## Three Player Mode

Player 1 → Victim

Player 2 → Perpetrator

Player 3 → Investigator

Every player has limited information.

Nobody knows the complete truth.
